About Us


Everyone has the brain power to follow the stock market!


ShareTrade crowdsources earnings and economic estimates from 69,325 hedge fund, brokerage, independent and amateur analysts. By collecting estimates from a diverse community of individuals, we've built a highly accurate and representative data set of true market expectations. The ShareTrade Consensus is consistently more accurate and robust than traditional data sources of Wall Street consensus like Thomson Reuters I/B/E/S. And while 70% of companies beat their Wall Street consensus, only about half will beat ShareTrade in a given quarter.
